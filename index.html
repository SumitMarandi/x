This website helps you to find the neccessary drug/ medicine in your area. This is the Government of India initative to give you access to all the medicine available in the country.

This portal contains the information of all the medicine. You can search for your medicine and find the nearest pharmacy where you can find it.

    Our database has:

  • All types of drugs & medicine record.
  • Every pharmacy & drug store present in the country. (with there contact details in it.)
  • Details about the medicine.
  • And many more...

If you need blood in medical emergeny. Don't worry, we got you covered. You can search for your required blood group and find nearest blood bank that has it.

You can also get contact details of the blood bank.

This portal is being run by Government of India and It is completely free to use.
